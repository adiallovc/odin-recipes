Black Bean Burger



Description

A flavorful and hearty vegetarian alternative to a traditional burger, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. These burgers are simple to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings.
Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 10 minutes
Servings: 2-3 patties

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

In a medium bowl, mash the black beans with a fork until they are mostly broken down but still have some texture.
Add the breadcrumbs, red onion, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well. The mixture should be firm enough to form into a patty. If it's too wet, add a little more breadcrumbs.
Form the mixture into two to three patties.
Cook the patties in a lightly oiled pan over medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and heated through. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings.
